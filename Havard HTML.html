Heading: Definition of Food

Food is a substance, consisting essentially of protein, carbohydrate, fat, and other nutrients used in the body of an organism to sustain growth and vital processes to sustain growth and vital processes and to furnish energy.

Plants which convert solar energy to food by photosynthesis are the primary food source. To learn more about food please stay tuned.
